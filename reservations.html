Reservation Policy

  • Reservations are available up to 30 days in advance.
  • We hold reservations for 15 minutes past the scheduled time.
  • For parties of 6 or more, a credit card is required to hold the reservation.
  • Cancellations must be made at least 24 hours in advance.
  • For special dietary needs, please note in your reservation or contact us directly.
