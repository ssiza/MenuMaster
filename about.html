Our Story

Passion for Culinary Excellence

Gourmet Haven was founded in 2010 by Chef Michael Rodriguez, whose passion for culinary arts began in his grandmother's kitchen in Naples, Italy. After training at the Culinary Institute of America and working in Michelin-starred restaurants across Europe, Chef Rodriguez returned to his hometown to create a restaurant that would blend traditional techniques with innovative flavors.

What started as a small, intimate dining spot has grown into one of the city's most beloved culinary destinations. Our philosophy is simple: use the freshest ingredients, prepare them with care, and serve them with love.
